Frequently Asked Questions

How does the free trial work?

You get full access to all SafeGuardAI features for 30 days with no credit card required. After the trial, you can choose to continue with a paid subscription or cancel anytime.

Is my family's data really private?

Yes. All analysis happens locally on your devices. Personal messages and content never leave your family's devices. We use advanced privacy-preserving technologies to ensure your data stays yours.

Can schools or healthcare providers use SafeGuardAI?

Absolutely! We have specialized solutions for educational institutions and healthcare providers. Contact us to learn about our institutional plans and integration options.

What kind of support do you offer?

We provide comprehensive onboarding support, regular webinars, a resource library, and direct support through email and chat. Our team is here to help you succeed.
