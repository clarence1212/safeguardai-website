Our Mission and Team

SafeGuardAI was founded to help families navigate digital life in a healthy, balanced way.

We believe technology should support family relationships, not strain them. Our team combines expertise in technology, psychology, and family wellness to create tools that are both effective and respectful of privacy and family autonomy.
